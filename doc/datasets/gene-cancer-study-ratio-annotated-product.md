## Format Decision

The default format remains Feather or Parquet-compatible tabular output plus explicit
metadata documentation. This product should not be converted to AnnData unless a
consumer needs matrix semantics with observation and feature metadata.

## Source-Fitness Role

For pre-cancer work, this product can support molecular questions about mutation
frequency, driver carriage, cancer-label context, and cross-sectional mutation
ordering where sample metadata identifies precursor or invasive state. It does not
support population prevalence or clinical progression rates without external cohort,
registry, or screening denominators.
