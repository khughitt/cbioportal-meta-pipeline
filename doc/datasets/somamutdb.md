# SomaMutDB

## Summary

SomaMutDB (Yaacov 2023) — a curated database of somatic mutations identified in normal human tissues aggregated across published studies; used as a normal-tissue somatic-mutation reference.

## Access and Scope

- Accessions: https://vijglab.einsteinmed.org/SomaMutDB
- Source URL: https://vijglab.einsteinmed.org/SomaMutDB
- Organism/population: Homo sapiens (normal tissue)
- Modality: curated normal-tissue somatic-mutation database
- Sample size: ~3.5M mutations across tissues
