# MSK-MET

## Summary

MSK MetTropism (Nguyen 2022) — ~25,000 patients with MSK-IMPACT tumor sequencing and curated metastatic-site annotations, used to study organotropism and metastatic burden across cancer types.

## Access and Scope

- Accessions: cBioPortal msk_met_2021
- Source URL: https://www.cbioportal.org/study/summary?id=msk_met_2021
- Organism/population: Homo sapiens
- Modality: targeted-panel sequencing + metastasis annotation
- Sample size: ~25,000 patients, ~50 cancer types
