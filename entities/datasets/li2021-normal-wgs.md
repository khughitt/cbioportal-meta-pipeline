# Li 2021 normal multi-organ WGS cohort

## Summary

Li et al. 2021 (Nature) whole-genome sequencing of laser-microdissected normal epithelial crypts/glands across nine GI and respiratory tissues (donors PN1–PN9). Raw data deposited under EGA accession EGAD00001007859; the project derives the normal-tissue SBS spectra product from its supplementary tables.

## Access and Scope

- Accessions: EGA:EGAD00001007859
- Source URL: https://ega-archive.org/datasets/EGAD00001007859
- Organism/population: Homo sapiens (normal tissue)
- Modality: whole-genome sequencing (microdissected)
- Sample size: 9 donors, 9 tissue types
