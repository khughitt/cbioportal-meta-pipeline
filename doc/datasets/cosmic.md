# COSMIC

## Summary

Catalogue Of Somatic Mutations In Cancer (Tate 2019) — the curated reference catalogue of somatic mutations and cancer genes (Cancer Gene Census) aggregated from the literature and large sequencing screens.

## Access and Scope

- Accessions: https://cancer.sanger.ac.uk/cosmic
- Source URL: https://cancer.sanger.ac.uk/cosmic
- Organism/population: Homo sapiens
- Modality: curated somatic-mutation catalogue
- Sample size: millions of curated coding mutations
